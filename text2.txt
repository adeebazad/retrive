The Institution is located in Vivek Vihar Phase II, in east Delhi. It has state-of-the-art infrastructure, houses over a thousand students at present, and is committed to quality and excellence. Further the institution aims at facilitating students to become highly competitive at the global level and instil lthe values and work ethics that enable them to be responsible and proud citizens of the country. The institution runs programs that enable learning in the field of Business Administration/Management, Social Sciences and Entrepreneurship, and also houses the Entrepreneurship Development Centre, that aims at being a hub for initiating, supporting and facilitating, entrepreneurship, and related learning, venture development and research.
